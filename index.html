Mind Over Colour

Prepare to put your perception skills to the test in this mind-bending game: Mind Over Colour. You'll be presented with colourful words and given four options to choose from. Your task is to select the word that matches the given colour, while completely ignoring the color of the word itself. Sounds tricky, right? Every correct answer will earn you an extra second on the clock, giving you more time to tackle the next challenge.

But beware! For every wrong answer, one precious second will be deducted from your remaining time. So be careful and think quickly!
