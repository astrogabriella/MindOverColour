Mind Over Colour

Prepare to put your perception skills to the test in this mind–bending game: Mind Over Colour. You'll be presented with colourful words and given four options to choose from. Your task is to select the word that matches the given color, while completely ignoring the color of the word itself. Sounds tricky, right? Sharpen your skills and try to beat your high score.

But beware! For every wrong answer, one precious second will be deducted from your remaining time. So stay focused, trust your instincts, and strive for a new record!!
